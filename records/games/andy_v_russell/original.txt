
This is the first recorded HW game that I know of.
It was played by email December 2003 to March 2004.
The email exchange is described here:
http://www.wunderland.com/WTS/Andy/Games/ILoveHomeworlds.html#PlayingWithRussell

The notation they chose isn't used anywhere else,
but I think I've got it figured out, and I've translated it into record.hwl and record.sdg


Andy's home is mistakenly referred to as MYSB in turns 5 and 9.
